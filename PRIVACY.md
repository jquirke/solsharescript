# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Overview

SolarSlice is a personal energy monitoring app that displays solar and electricity data from your Allume Energy SolCentre account. This privacy policy describes how the app handles your information.

## Data We Collect

SolarSlice does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party.

## Credentials

Your Allume Energy email address and password are:
- Stored exclusively on your device in the iOS Keychain
- Used only to authenticate with the Allume Energy SolCentre API
- Never transmitted to any server other than the Allume Energy API (`api.allumeenergy.com.au`)
- Never shared with the developer or any third party

## Energy Data

Solar and electricity data fetched from the Allume Energy API is:
- Displayed within the app and cached temporarily in device memory
- Stored locally on your device via App Groups to power the home screen widget
- Never transmitted to the developer or any third party

## Third-Party Services

SolarSlice communicates exclusively with the Allume Energy SolCentre API to retrieve your energy data. Please refer to [Allume Energy's privacy policy](https://allumeenergy.com.au) for information on how they handle your data.

## Analytics and Tracking

SolarSlice does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs. No data about your usage of the app is collected.

## Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
